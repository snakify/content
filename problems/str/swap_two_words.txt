Name:
To swap the two words

Statement:
<p>Given a string consisting of exactly two words separated by a space. 
Print a new string with the first and second word positions swapped (the second word is printed first).

<p>This task should not use loops and <code>if</code>.


Test:
Hello, world!

Answer:
world! Hello,


Test:
A B

Answer:
B A


Test:
Q WERRTYUIOP

Answer:
WERRTYUIOP Q


Test:
QWERTYUIOP M

Answer:
M QWERTYUIOP

sadfahsjkldfhasjkdfhaklsjdfhjkl asdlkfhasdjklfhaslkdfjhalskdfgalsdf
